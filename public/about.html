About

Kenyon Bertram

Bertram Home Inspections, located in Southern New Hampshire, works diligently and honestly to meet your home inspection needs. The company, operated by Kenyon Bertram, will provide a complete home inspection prior to purchasing the home of your dreams. Kenyon only schedules one home inspection per day to ensure you receive his full attention and a thorough inspection. His services include general home inspections, Radon testing, and water quality testing. If other services are needed such as a septic inspection, he can arrange that as well. You can even accompany Kenyon during the inspection so he can answer any questions or concerns you have about your new home. After the inspection, he will provide you with a full written report with color photographs that will be emailed to you within 24 hours. This ensures you have a full understanding of any problems or maintenance items before your purchase is finalized.

Thank you for reaching out. We'll be in touch soon.
